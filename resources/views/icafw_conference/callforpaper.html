INTERNATIONAL CONFERENCE ON AI & FUTURE OF WORKS (ICAFW)

Call for Papers

The International Conference on AI & Future of Work (ICAFW) invites researchers, academics, and industry professionals to submit papers on topics related to AI and the future of work. We welcome contributions that explore innovative solutions, cutting-edge research, and practical applications in the following thematic areas:

  1. AI Integration in Diverse Sectors

    Explore AI applications and advancements in key sectors such as finance, retail, agriculture, healthcare, education, climate change, legal, and more.

  2. Data-Driven Insights and Ethics

    Delve into the role of data and ethics in AI, ensuring responsible and transparent data-driven decision-making.

  3. Technological Innovations and Use Cases

    Showcase emerging technologies and their applications through real-world use cases presented by industry pioneers.

  4. Policy, Regulation, and Governance

    Discuss the imperative need for informed policies and regulations to guide the responsible adoption and implementation of AI technologies.

  5. Giga Economy & Freelancing, Diversity, Equity, & Inclusion, Remote Work & Virtual Collaboration, Cybersecurity, Digital Transformation, and more.

    Explore topics related to the gig economy, freelancing, diversity, equity, inclusion, remote work, virtual collaboration, cybersecurity, digital transformation, and other relevant areas.
